Campus Ambassador Program is an opportunity for students to act as liaisons between his/her college and Engineer'14 .
It aims at connecting all the colleges in India with technology and Innovations.

Campus Ambassador will have responsibilities pertaining to Engineer'14 at his/her alma mater. Following are the responsibilities :

  • To post Engineer'14 links on their departmental/college/university groups on facebook and other social networking sites.
  • Posters will be sent through courier which are to be put at its correct place like notice boards, etc.
  • Give regular updates to our Publicity Team about the status of ENGINEER awareness in your respective college and discuss necessary publicity strategy.

Following are the benefits/incentives that we will be providing to our Campus Ambassadors :

  • 50% discount in all workshops
  • free accomodation
  • Free entry to all pro shows
  • Engineer T-shirt
  • Engineer goodies
  • other coupons worth Rs. 200
  • Free recharge coupon of Rs.100
All these benefits will be given only when atleast 10 students from that college participates in offline events.

Just like any member of our exclusive team here, we treat our Campus Ambassador with the same respect and trust that a core member enjoys because it takes one to know one.
