Hospitality

Guidelines

  • All participants should carry their College ID.
  • Please ensure that you have received a confirmation mail before setting out.
  • Any changes in your schedule of arrival/departure must be reported well in advance to the ENGINEER Hospitality Team.
  • Participants for more than one event should register for accommodation once only. Registration should be done along with the team they want to stay with.
  • The Team Leader should complete all the formalities on behalf of the whole group. The Team Leader must report at the Accommodation Desk with the hard copy of the confirmation mail and College IDs of all members of the team and collect their Registration and Accommodation IDs.
  • Due to space constraint in NITK Campus, accommodation availability is limited. Hence, accommodation is provided on first come first serve basis. Even after receiving confirmation, your accommodation will be held booked only for 1 day in case of delay in arrival.
  • The participants are advised to stay strictly for the same number of days for which they have paid. Any Team/participant not abiding by this rule would be liable to security action and a fine of Rs 1000 for an extra day would be charged (per person in case of team).
  • Any unlawful act would be dealt with very strictly with a possible interference of local police.
  • Your number of days will be calculated on a 24 hr. basis depending on your time of arrival and departure.

FAQ's

Q. Shall I carry my id card with me ?
A. It is mandatory for all participants to carry college IDs. This is for your own convenience.      Moreover you will be asked to produce your college id cards at the time of registration.
Q. What should I do after coming to the campus ?
A. Outstation participants who have registered for accommodation are requested to report to the      ENGINEER office from where they shall be directed to the Hospitality Desk where the required   formalities involving checking of documents, allotment of rooms will be finished.
Q. What kind of accommodation is provided ?
A. Accommodation is provided on a sharing basis. We have common lounges and halls in each      hostel. Mattresses and other essential commodities will be provided.
