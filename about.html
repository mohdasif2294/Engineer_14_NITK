About ENGINEER

Think Create ENGINEER is the Annual Technical Symposium of NITK Surathkal and is one of the largest of its kind in India. Started in 2005 as an amalgamation of many technical fests held in NITK Surathkal by its various student clubs, ENGINEER has grown in leaps and bounds over the years. It is held over 4 days in the second half of the month of October and has consistently attracted participants from all over India.

All four days of ENGINEER are encompassed with lucid events ranging from Quizzes, war of machines, coding and business challenges and paper presentations. These are broadly classified into various Branch Events. Apart from this, Workshops in vivid fields covering all aspects of present day technical madness are held too. To upkeep the environmentalists in engineers, social/environmental awareness initiatives are undertaken under the banner of SCE (Socially Conscious Engineering). Apart from the events the fest sees main shows which portrays the adherence of entertainment to technology.
