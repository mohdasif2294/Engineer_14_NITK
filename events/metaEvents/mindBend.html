Mind bend is a fun quiz for those who have an undying thirst to know more about materials. It tests the participant's knowledge of obscure and unnoticed facts about materials in an interactive and fascinating new format. In the previous edition of Engineer it was organized in the following format:-

  • Prelims round consisting of crossword and short questionnaire.
  • Final round between 6 teams
