The event deals with solving case studies of corporate companies. The participants were provided with a problem statement from Intuit last year. Three case studies are provided. Participants can choose any one of these case studies. Participants will have to present their solutions in the form of B-Plan presentation. The solution that fits the most with the company would be selected and the team would be provided with a cash prize.
