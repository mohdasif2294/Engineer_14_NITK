At Engineer, Symphony gives the participants an opportunity to dabble into the sights and sounds of electronics and bring the subject to life. Symphony requires participants to control a musical fountain’s water flow and lights, so that the fountain dances to the tunes of the music. A huge draw every Engineer, Symphony generates huge participation and promises a great crowd.
